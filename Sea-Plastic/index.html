About US


Make our 'HOME' Beautiful!

We are high school students, who strive to make students life easier, calmer, and help them find balance , which we think is one of the hardest element ever to succeed at in student's life. Not only that, we neglect on our physical and mental health, therefore we provide free courses which not only teaches them how to have balance, but also how they can live a healthier life in terms of physical and mental.I attended the University of British Columbia, Canada where I completed a five-year program for a BSc in Dietetics and graduated with honours. This included a 10-month dietetic internship I completed at Vancouver Coastal Health including rotations at Vancouver General Hospital and Lions Gate Hospital. All Dietitians must have this training to become registered to practice in the province of British Columbia, Canada.

By learning more about Pick Up Limes you are joining the movement towards better health. Above all, we firmly believe that anyone and everyone can master this just by joining and following our courses. We hope we can change your life today. Join us today!
